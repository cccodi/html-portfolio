Movie Reviews 2025 by Clarisse

Recently watched movies so far this 2025

The Final Destination: The Bloodline

It was bloody as hell and very gruesome scenes everywhere. And it's all because of a coin and a stupid child lol. I don't recommend watching it while other people were sleeping beside you because you might wake them with your screams. Tbh, I prefer the first final destination movie.

Insidious

Too scary. I don't want to watch it ever again. And I won't be able to watch it alone ever.

Bubble

It was a cute fantasy anime movie. The animations was amazing and the sports the characters play was dangerously cool. It has good plot and justa lighthearted movie you can watch.

Kpop Demon Hunters

It's trending recently and Jona recommended it so I watched it. Animations are cool and it's easy to watch. It was just okay tho.
